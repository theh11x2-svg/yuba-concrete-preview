FAQ

Answers Before
You Even Ask

Got a question we didn't cover? Call (530) 561-6082 — real person, no phone tree.

How much does a concrete driveway cost?

Most residential driveways in the Yuba City area run $8–$15 per square foot depending on thickness, rebar, finish type, and site prep. A typical 500 sq ft driveway lands between $4,000–$7,500. We'll give you an exact written quote after a free on-site measure.

How long does a typical project take?

A standard driveway takes 2–4 days from demo to pour. Patios run 1–3 days. Concrete needs 7 days before heavy use and 28 days to reach full strength. We'll give you a clear timeline up front and stick to it.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed with the California Contractors State License Board (CSLB), plus general liability and workers' comp insurance. We'll send copies of our certificates with every quote so you know you're covered.

Do you offer warranties?

Every job comes with a 5-year workmanship warranty in writing. If a crack develops from our workmanship — not ground movement or Acts of God — we fix it at no charge. The manufacturer's warranty on materials is separate and typically runs longer.

Can you do decorative concrete?

Absolutely. Stamped, stained, colored, exposed aggregate, polished, salt finish, broom finish — we do it all. Bring us a photo of what you like and we'll match it.

Do you do repairs or only new installs?

Both. We handle crack repair, resurfacing, slab jacking, spall patches, and full replacements. If your existing concrete still has good structure, repair is almost always cheaper than replacement — and we'll tell you honestly which option makes sense.
